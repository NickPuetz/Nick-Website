Who Are We?

Puetz Property Solutions, LLC is a small, family-run company focused on improving communities and improving lives. We believe that everybody deserves a comfortable place to live, and that's our goal. We believe we can do this by renovating run-down houses while also keeping housing prices affordable for everyone. Mortgages and rent can cause a strain on your budget, but at Puetz Property Solutions, we believe that we can get everyone into a house that they are proud of while still keeping an eye on the wallet.

Why Work With Us?

Why us? There are plenty of companies that rent houses. There are plenty of companies tht sell houses. What sets us apart is our dedication to the people. We truly believe that what we do improves peoples lives. As a small private company, we are able to keep our focus on doing what is right. We are able to make decisions not based off of money, but helping. This is what sets us apart and what makes us different.

What's Next?

Deciding what is next for a company is a tough thing to do. Here at Puetz Property Solutions, we want to continue to help people and to expand into new markets. In the future, we see ourselves taking on the Minneapolis and Rochester, Minnesota housing markets. We want to continue to help people find affordable housing that they can call home.
